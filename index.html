How it Works

Online Quiz Test

Registration to App

Students can download the app from app store and register themselves with IGS App. A user password will be sent to their given email account.

Student Login

Students can login by using their user id and password. Post login, they can attend quiz test.

Quiz Test Process

Students have to attempt 10 questions to win the quiz test. They can perform this test daily 4 times to win the prizes.

Result

From each test 1st, 2nd and 3rd winner will be selected and winning prizes will be sent to their account daily.
